— For you, right now —
Three questions to sit with
    — An honest look at yourself —

    Look at your four scores. No rush. Just notice what's true for you right now.

    On balance
    No one has all four areas thriving at the same time — and that's okay. What matters is noticing when one area has been quietly neglected for too long. This is your mirror.
    Take these with you
    • Which area are you most satisfied with right now? Which one bothers you the most?
    • Is there an area where you already know something needs to change — but you've been avoiding it?
    • What's the uncomfortable truth this reflection is showing you?
    • If you did this again in three months, which area do you hope would look different?
